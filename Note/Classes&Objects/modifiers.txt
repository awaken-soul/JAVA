We have public,private,package-private,protected modifiers.
These allow to control what clases can access the member of your class.
public classes can be accessed from other classes. They are least suggested to use unless for say some constants.
private classes are ones who can only be accessed in its own class.
protected keeps access the class within the same package or its subclass in another package.
package private protects the access by only providing the access within the package.
